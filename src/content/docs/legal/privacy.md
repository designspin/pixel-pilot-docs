# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 12, 2025

## Overview

Pixel Pilot is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Pixel Pilot:
- Does not track your usage
- Does not collect analytics
- Does not require an account
- Does not transmit your artwork or projects

## Local Storage

All your work is stored locally on your device:
- Project files are saved where you choose
- Preferences are stored in standard macOS user preferences
- No data is sent to external servers

## Third-Party Services

Pixel Pilot does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

As we do not collect any data, Pixel Pilot is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our [Support page](/legal/support/).
