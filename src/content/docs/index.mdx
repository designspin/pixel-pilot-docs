## Why Pixel Pilot?

Pixel Pilot is designed for pixel artists who want a fast, native macOS experience. Whether you're creating game sprites, animated characters, or retro art, Pixel Pilot gives you the tools you need without the bloat.

**Built for macOS.** Native performance, familiar interface, and full system integration including dark mode, keyboard shortcuts, and VoiceOver support.

## Perfect For

- **Game Developers** - Create sprites and animations for 2D games
- **Pixel Artists** - Express your creativity with dedicated pixel art tools
- **Indie Creators** - Affordable, powerful tools without subscription fees
- **Retro Enthusiasts** - Bring back the aesthetic of classic pixel art

## System Requirements

- macOS 12.0 or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel Mac
- 100MB free disk space
